A typical black box penetration test takes 1-2 weeks per application. White box tests with authenticated access generally take 2-3 weeks depending on the complexity of the application. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial scoping call.

We support HIPAA, PCI DSS, SOC2, and other major compliance frameworks. Our penetration testing reports are designed to satisfy auditor requirements for these standards, and our virtual CISO service can guide you through the full compliance process.

A Virtual CISO provides executive-level security leadership on a fractional basis. If your company handles sensitive data, needs to meet compliance requirements, or is growing beyond the point where ad-hoc security is sufficient, a vCISO can build your security program without the overhead of a full-time hire.
