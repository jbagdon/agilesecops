Consumers want more IT Security

April 16, 2018

A recent poll, performed by IBM, showed that consumers where lacking trust in companies and wanted more Cyber Security to protect the products they are buying. I'm not sure what products are on the consumer's mind when they were asked these questions. However, I would guess they ranged from online SaaS services to the Internet of Things (IoT).

Reading this survey really struck home. When I purchased my current home a couple of years ago ADT wanted me to update my current security system with their new Internet-connected Pulse system. When I started asking questions to the sales representative about how they secure their product from hackers I got a blank stare. I asked if they performed any penetration testing or had any certifications from any well known IT Security organizations in regards to the security of their own system. He didn't know but put me in touch with their support team who told me that I wasn't supposed to be talking directly to them. As you can imagine, without getting the satisfaction of knowing the system that I was being offered was secured, I didn't buy it.

I believe that more people are starting to realize the importance of IT Security in the products of the marketplace. Business need to step it up a notch and add cybersecurity to their products in order to protect customers data and safety.
